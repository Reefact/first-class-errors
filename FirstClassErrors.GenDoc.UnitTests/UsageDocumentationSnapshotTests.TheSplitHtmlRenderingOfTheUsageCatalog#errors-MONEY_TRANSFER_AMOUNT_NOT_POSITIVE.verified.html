MONEY_TRANSFER_AMOUNT_NOT_POSITIVE #

Non-positive transfer amount

Documentation

This error occurs when a money transfer is requested with an amount that is zero or negative.

Business rule: A money transfer amount must be strictly positive.

Diagnostics

Possible causeOriginAnalysis hint
The amount was entered or computed as zero or a negative value.ExternalCheck the requested transfer amount and confirm it is greater than zero.
